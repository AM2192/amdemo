History

A few years ago before Covid-19 got large, three friends started a new hobby for themselves. Honestly it we have admit that it got out of our hands. We had lots of round on golfcourses and our skills get better. We deside that we want more experiences from golf. We wanted to take a roadtrip and at the same time play golf as much as we can.

Where would be more beautiful landscape than Åland. So that was decided, we were heading to Åland at summer 2020. In Åland there is two golf courses, Ålands Golfklubb next to beautiful nature and historic landscape of castle Kastelholma. The second course is smaller and located at Eckerö. At this trip we did focused playing only at Ålands Golfklubb.
